About Me

Hi there, I’m Google👋, and I help students and professionals unlock their potential in the exciting world of technology.Google's mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. We were founded at Stanford University in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. Initially, we were a search engine called Backrub, but we later changed the name to Google, inspired by the mathematical term "googol". Since then, we've expanded into various products and services, including maps, email, and cloud computing.
