A little history

Organizing information since 1998

Google began as a Stanford University research project by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. Its name was inspired by “googol,” the mathematical term for a 1 followed by 100 zeros.

What started as a search engine has grown into a family of products spanning maps, email, video, productivity, cloud computing, and more.
